    Urban, independent, contemporary, centric, unrepeatable, unique… We have been tagged in many ways but, however, none of them can completely describe us. There is nothing comparable to experience Caro Hotel firsthand: 26 comfortable and inviting rooms, each different from the others, and almost 40 people in our staff doing their best to provide a relaxed and impeccable service…

    A one of a kind restaurant…

    Romans, Arabs, Christians… the gastronomic history of Valencia is a history of contrasts and nuances. How would it be to recall all this culinary inheritance with the creativity and techniques from the 21st Century?. How would it be to do it with a playful and memorable gastronomic proposition?.

    In a space highlighted by German editors Taschen as one of the most stunning restaurants in the world and managed by our former elBulli chef Miguel Ángel Mayor, Sucede is our answer.
