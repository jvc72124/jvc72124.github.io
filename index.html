The Best of Spain

The official name of Spain is Reino de España and the official language is Castilian Spanish. The capital of Spain is Madrid and it is located near the center of the country. The country of Spain is located in Western Europe, to the southwest of France. Spain has a very diverse landscape consisting of mountains, beaches, plains, and cliffs. Spain is especially known for its excellent food and wine, and some of the world’s most renowned restaurants are found inside Spain’s borders. One famous type of Spanish cuisine is tapas. Tapas are small plates that come in many different forms. Some of the most famous and popular Spanish tapas include Jamón, Queso y Chorizo, Tortilla Española, and Patatas Bravas. There are hundreds of things to do an see in Spain; some of the popular things include Roman ruins, grand cathedrals, incredible museums and various monuments. The best time to visit varies based off where in Spain you would like to visit.

Sights to See in Spain

  • The Alhambra: Located in Granada, Spain, the Alhambra is an incredible palace and fortress built on a plateau that overlooks the city. It is considered by some to be the 8th wonder of the world. It was built by the first Nasrid king named Ibn el Ahmar. Around 2 million people visit the Alhambra every year.
  • Mezquita de Córdoba: This is a great cathedral and mosque that can be found in Córdoba, Spain. Many believe this is the best example of Muslim design in all of Spain. It is admired for its incredible décor marble floors, giant arches and Byzantine mosaics.
  • Costa del Sol: This coast of Spain is located in the Provincia de Málaga. It is the southernmost edge of Spain and has a variety of recreational, historic and cultural activities.
  • Templo Expiatorio de la Sagrada Familia: One can find this incredible temple in Barcelona. The construction of the Sagrada Familia began in 1882 and it is still not complete. It was designed by the famous architect Antoni Gaudi. This is an incredibly popular attraction for those visiting Spain, and it is an amazing and elaborate piece of architecture.
