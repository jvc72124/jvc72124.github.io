El Canalla

El Canalla is our restaurant in Sarrià. It is a wine cellar with a lot of character, serving tapas and snacks and located in the same square as the church in Sarrià.

Cuisine that is locally sourced, market-based and Mediterranean. We have a sunny terrace where you can enjoy the best wines and our “El Villano” lounge – a venue for your private meals and events.

Mayor de Sarriá 95
08017, Barcelona
Tel: +34 93 205 88 06
elcanalla@gruposantelmo.com
